About Me

My name is Chengzhi("chen-gee") Zhang. I was born in picturesque Hangzhou city in China. Due to some random walk, I am now a Ph.D. candidate at Georgia Tech working with Professor Brian Magerko at Expressive Machinery Lab. Before that, I earned my M.S. in Computational Design at at Carnegie Mellon University, and my Bachelor's in Industrial Design at Hunan University, China.

My research interests include:
  • Enhancing the public's AI literacy in informal learning setting, with a focus on generative AI (e.g., ChatGPT)
  • Exploring the way to best leveraging AI to enhance the user experience
  • Discovering embodied and tangible ways of interacting and improvising with computers

In my free time, I love to cook, explore local cafes, play with my cat, and go to the gym.
